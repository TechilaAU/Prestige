Who we are

Local, licensed,
and proud of the finish.

Prestige Concreting Whitsundays is a hands-on, locally owned concreting business. You deal with Brad from the first call to the final clean-up — no middlemen, no runaround.

The business

Run by a concreter,
not a call centre.

Prestige Concreting was built on a simple idea: do honest, quality concreting for people in the Whitsundays and back it up with a finish you can be proud of. Brad runs every job personally — quoting, prepping and finishing — so nothing gets lost between the office and the site.

From a single backyard slab to a full commercial pour, the standard doesn't change. Set it out right, get the prep and steel correct, and finish it clean. That's what makes concrete last — and what makes it look prestige.
