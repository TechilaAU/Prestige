01 · Driveways

Driveways

A driveway is the first thing people see and the most-used slab on the block. We form, reinforce and finish driveways that stay level, drain right and handle the heavy stuff.

  • Plain, coloured or exposed finishes
  • Correct fall for drainage
  • Reinforced for vehicle loads
02 · Slabs

House & Shed Slabs

Slabs and footings set out to your engineer's plans — house slabs, shed slabs, granny flats and extensions. Proper prep, steel and vapour barrier so what goes on top lasts.

  • Set out to engineering
  • Steel fixed & certified
  • Residential & rural
03 · Outdoor

Patios & Alfresco

Outdoor living areas, alfresco slabs and entertaining spaces that tie in cleanly with the house. Get the levels and finish right and it becomes the best room on the property.

  • Matched to house levels
  • Smooth or textured finish
  • Ready for roofing or pavers

Pathways & Footpaths

Paths, footpaths and side access that connect the property and keep the mud out. Neat edges, even widths and a non-slip finish underfoot.

  • Clean, consistent edging
  • Non-slip broom finish
  • Council-spec available
05 · Floors

Shed & Garage Floors

Hard-wearing floors for sheds, garages and workshops — flat, level and ready for whatever rolls in. Optional sealing for a cleaner, dust-free surface.

  • Dead-flat, level finish
  • Sealed or polished options
  • Rated for machinery
06 · Structure

Retaining & Kerbing

Retaining footings, concrete kerbing and edge restraints to hold ground, define driveways and keep gardens where they belong on sloping Whitsunday blocks.

  • Built for slope & runoff

Good to know

Common questions.

Do you give free quotes? +
Yes. Brad comes to site, measures up and gives you a clear written quote with no obligation. We'd rather see the job in person than guess over the phone.
What areas do you cover? +
The whole Whitsunday region and surrounds — Cannonvale, Airlie Beach, Proserpine and out to Mackay in the south and Bowen in the north. Not sure if you're in the patch? Give us a call.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Both. We hold QBCC licence 15008969 and carry full insurance on every job, residential or commercial. Happy to provide details on request.
Do you do both small jobs and big commercial pours? +
We do. A backyard path and a commercial hardstand get the same prep and finish — the difference is just scale. Tell us what you need and we'll sort it.
Can you colour-match or do a decorative finish? +
Absolutely — coloured, stencilled and exposed aggregate are some of our most popular finishes. Bring a photo or an idea and we'll talk through the options.
